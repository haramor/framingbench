## Warranty 1

**Best for Customization** | Forbes Advisor Rating: 4.3/5

Forbes Home's analysis found that Warranty 1 earns a spot on our best home warranty providers list for its ample add-on options with a 4.3 star rating. Warranty 1 allows customers to customize their service with an excess of add-ons compared to the competition (including niche coverages like plumbing and home security systems) while staying on budget. Plus, Warranty 1's high aggregate coverage limit of $25,000 is helpful for people with more expensive items or frequent claims.

Warranty 1 has three service plans. The basic one, Simply Kitchen, focuses on coverage of kitchen appliances. Complete Home expands on that with the inclusion of heating, cooling, plumbing and electrical. The premium plan, Pinnacle Home, has "supreme upgrades" and includes washers and dryers.

In an industry where critical reviews are already common, Warranty 1 stands out for incredibly negative customer sentiments. Complaints stretch across every aspect of the customer journey. Many say it's hard to get in contact with customer service, technicians are incompetent, issues are left unresolved and claims are denied.

**Pros:** High aggregate coverage limits; Military discounts; Option for low service fees

**Cons:** Highly critical reviews; Limited customer service options and availability; Confusing service terms, specifically regarding cancellation fees
