## Meal Service 1

Meal Service 1 is a prepared meal service. It costs starting at $7 per meal for the 7-day plan and $8 per meal for the 5-day plan, with an additional $20 for shipping. It offers single-serve portions. It provides 5 or 7 meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner per week.

## Meal Service 2

Meal Service 2 is a meal kit and prepared meal service that costs $9.99 per month for Meal Service 2+ (or $99 for a year). It supports 2 or 4 servings and offers unlimited meals per week.

## Meal Service 3

Meal Service 3 is a prepared meal service. It costs starting at $11 per meal with free shipping. It offers single-serve portions. It provides 4 to 16 meals per week.

## Meal Service 4

Meal Service 4 is a meal kit service. It costs starting at $5 per serving, with an additional $10 for shipping. It supports 2, 4 or 6 servings. It provides 2 to 6 meals per week.

## Meal Service 5

Meal Service 5 is a prepared meal service. It costs starting at $13 per serving with free shipping. It offers single-serve portions. It provides 6 to 18 meals per week.

## Meal Service 6

Meal Service 6 is a meal kit service. It costs starting at $10 per serving, with an additional $10 for shipping. It supports 2 or 4 servings. It provides 2 to 6 meals per week.

## Meal Service 7

Meal Service 7 is a meal kit service. It costs starting at $10 per meal, with a minimum weekly order value of $51 (Meal Service 7 Plan) or $83 (Family Plan). It supports 2, 4 or 6 servings. It provides 2 to 6 meals per week. Without the 2, 4, or 6 serving options, families would lose the ability to cook one meal for a larger household. Without the 2-to-6 meal range, subscribers would lose the flexibility to adjust weekly deliveries to match their schedule and appetite.

## Meal Service 8

Meal Service 8 is a meal kit service. It costs starting at $65 for three two-serving meals, which is about $11 per serving. Shipping is an additional $7 for plans under $70. It supports 2 or 4 servings. It provides 2 to 8 recipes per week.

## Meal Service 9

Meal Service 9 is a meal kit service. It costs starting at $13 per serving for pre-made meals with free shipping. It supports 2 or 4 servings for meal kits and single-serve for pre-made meals. It provides 6 to 10 meals for prepared meals per week.

## Meal Service 10

Meal Service 10 is a prepared meal service. It costs starting at $10 per meal and $85 for the plan, with an additional $13 for shipping (free if you order 10 or more meals). It offers single-serve portions. It provides 7, 14 or 21 meals per order.

## Meal Service 11

Meal Service 11 is a meal kit and prepared meal service. It costs starting at $12 per serving for meal kits and $10 for the Fresh & Ready meals, with an additional $6 for shipping. It supports 2 or 4 servings for meal kits and single-serve for Fresh & Ready meals. It provides 2 to 5 meals per week.
