## Bizee

**Best Essentials**

Bizee (formerly Incfile) is an online legal service that specializes in business formation. Bizee provides top-tier services at prices that are unmatched in the sector. Their most basic bundle costs customers $0 (plus state fees). Bizee provides registered agent and LLC creation services and accounting and compliance services, including drafting operating agreements, filing annual reports, and registering for EINs for your business entity. Thanks to their user-friendly design, you can purchase Bizee's services online. Guided snippets display on the screen when filling out the form, and a live chat box allows you to communicate with a representative. After our registration for Bizee legal services, a tax professional provided a free, one-hour consultation to address our legal issues. The fact that Bizee only offers a few services is their sole drawback. For example, they don't provide legal documents or legal consulting plans. Additionally, their customer service hours are limited and unavailable on weekends.

The Basic Package costs $0 plus state fees and includes filing of formation documents, business name searches, business bank accounts, access to online legal documents, and free registered agent service for the first year. The Standard Plan costs $199 plus state fees and adds banking resolution, tax consultations, ongoing legal support, EIN registration, an Operating Agreement, and legal document express shipping. The Premium Plan costs $299 plus state fees and adds business contracts, a business domain, hosting, personal documents, fast FedEx delivery, and a business formation kit. Registered Agent Service costs $99 per year after the first free year.

**Who it's for:** Entrepreneurs on a budget starting new businesses.

**Who it's not for:** Larger businesses or those needing in-depth legal advice.

**Venture Smarter Verdict:** Bizee offers a very affordable and straightforward business formation service, making it perfect for new entrepreneurs who are cost-sensitive.

**Pros:** Affordable legal coverage; Company filings are stored in digital formats; Automatic lifetime compliance notifications.

**Cons:** No option to retain a lawyer; Restricted intellectual property legal services.
