## NexusShield

NexusShield, formerly NexusFiling, is an online legal service for business formation. The Basic Package costs $0 plus state fees. It includes filing of formation documents, business name searches, business bank accounts, access to online legal documents, and free registered agent service for the first year. The Standard Plan costs $199 plus state fees. It adds banking resolution, tax consultations, ongoing legal support, EIN registration, an Operating Agreement, and legal document express shipping. The $299 plan adds business contracts, a business domain, hosting, personal documents, fast FedEx delivery, and a business formation kit. Registered Agent Service costs $99 per year after the first free year. NexusShield provides LLC creation, accounting, and compliance services. These include drafting operating agreements, filing annual reports, and registering for EINs. The platform includes guided snippets during form completion and a live chat function. After registration, a tax professional provides a free one-hour consultation. Customer service hours are limited and unavailable on weekends.

The $199 plan includes Starter plan features plus company alerts, business tax consultation, a business banking account, and a free year of registered agent service. The Small Business Legal Plan starts at $49 per month and offers designated consultations, tax audit services, collection letters, and business research advice. The Start A Business plan costs $149 plus state fees and includes name search and registration, LLC formation, and EIN registration. Trial Defense costs $9.95 per month and includes pre-trial counsel and advice. The individual monthly plan includes free legal document evaluations and consultations with licensed attorneys, as well as services for estate planning and mortgage documents. GuardianLaw provides counsel for traffic infractions, family law, and tax audits. A 25% discount on a firm's standard hourly costs is available for additional legal counsel. Coverage includes a client's spouse and dependent children. Clients can cancel at any time and receive a proportionate refund for unused days. Some benefits do not apply to pre-existing conditions.
