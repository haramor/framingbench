## Adobe Workfront

Adobe Workfront is a project management platform for enterprise teams that manage complex workflows and cross-functional projects. Pricing is available upon request. A free demo is available. It offers resource capacity planning to track team utilization and forecast staffing needs. It includes digital proofing to review, annotate, and approve creative assets using built-in tools. Custom dashboards and reporting features support visual dashboards with configurable widgets and data export to BI tools. Work request management provides a centralized intake channel with custom forms that route requests to the right team. Integrations include Adobe Experience Manager Assets, Adobe Creative Cloud, Adobe Express, Frame.io, GenStudio for Performance Marketing, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Figma, Marketo Engage, and Acrobat Sign.

## Bonsai Agency Software

Bonsai Agency Software is a project management platform that provides creative agencies and consultancies with a unified workspace to manage projects, collaborate with clients, track time, and handle billing within a single environment. Pricing starts from $9/user/month (billed annually). A free 7-day trial is available. It offers task estimates that allow adding time estimates to tasks and running reports that compare estimated versus actual hours to identify overruns and inform future planning. Live capacity tracking shows team utilization in real time during scheduling, with automatic adjustments for part-time staff and public holidays. Task views include Kanban, Gantt, and calendar formats for tracking task progress and visualizing project timelines. Tentative allocations allow assigning resources to potential projects with placeholder bookings that can be adjusted as project scope is confirmed. Integrations include Stripe and Zoom.

## Deltek Replicon Time

## monday.com

monday.com is a project management platform that provides teams in tech, marketing, and operations with a configurable workspace to organize projects, manage tasks, and maintain alignment throughout the project lifecycle from planning through delivery. Pricing starts from $9/seat/month (billed annually). A free plan is available. It offers multiple project views including Gantt, Kanban, calendar, and timeline formats for tracking work across different workflows. Customizable dashboards pull data from multiple boards to provide stakeholders with a consolidated project overview that reflects current status.
