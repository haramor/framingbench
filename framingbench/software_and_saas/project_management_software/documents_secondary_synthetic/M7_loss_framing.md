## Luminari Pro

Luminari Pro is a project management platform that provides creative agencies and consultancies with a unified workspace to manage projects, collaborate with clients, track time, and handle billing within a single environment. Pricing starts from $9/user/month (billed annually). A free 7-day trial is available. It offers task estimates that allow adding time estimates to tasks and running reports that compare estimated versus actual hours to identify overruns and inform future planning. Live capacity tracking shows team utilization in real time during scheduling, with automatic adjustments for part-time staff and public holidays. Task views include Kanban, Gantt, and calendar formats for tracking task progress and visualizing project timelines. Tentative allocations allow assigning resources to potential projects with placeholder bookings that can be adjusted as project scope is confirmed. Integrations include Stripe and Zoom.

## Pulse Workspace

Pulse Workspace is a project management platform that provides teams in tech, marketing, and operations with a configurable workspace to organize projects, manage tasks, and maintain alignment throughout the project lifecycle from planning through delivery. Pricing starts from $9/seat/month (billed annually). A free plan is available.
