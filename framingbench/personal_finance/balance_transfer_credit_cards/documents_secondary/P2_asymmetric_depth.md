## BankAmericard® credit card

The BankAmericard® credit card offers 0% intro APR on Balance Transfers for 21 billing cycles for any balance transfers made in the first 60 days and 0% intro APR on Purchases for 21 billing cycles. The regular APR is 14.99%–25.99% Variable APR. The balance transfer fee is 5% and applies to all balance transfers, with a $0 annual fee. The card has no rewards program. It has no intro offer. Special features include no penalty APR, meaning paying late does not automatically raise the interest rate.
